How to enroll my kid into a child care

Process you need to follow

Find a child care and contact it

- Choose a child care which meets your needs (location, quality standard, service type) using our map
- Contact or visit the child care and make sure that it has a place available 
- Prepare to enroll your child

Enrolling in child care

When enrolling your child you’ll need to provide the following information:
- Your child’s name and address and details of any special needs
- Information about yourself and any other parents, carers or guardians
- Details of the people who can pick up your child
- Information about your child’s medical health and immunisation status (an immunisation status certificate from showing your child is up to date with vaccinations for their age OR on a vaccine catch-up schedule OR has a medical condition preventing them from being fully vaccinated )

How do I obtain my child's immunisation status certificate?

Starting child care and apply for financial assistance

- Eligibility basics for child care subsidy (CCS)

1. Care for a child 13 or under and not attending secondary school, unless an exemption applies
2. Use an approved child care service
3. Be responsible for paying the child care fees
4. Meet residency and immunisation requirements

- Complete your Child Care Subsidy assessment with your Centrelink online account
